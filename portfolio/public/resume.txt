# Resume Placeholder

To add your actual resume PDF:

1. Generate or export your resume as a PDF
2. Place it in the `public/` folder as `resume.pdf`
3. Update the download link in the contact form if needed

The file is referenced at: `src/data/content.ts` → `personalData.resume`

When deployed, it will be accessible at: `https://yourdomain.com/resume.pdf`
